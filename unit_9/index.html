THE WELLNESS CODE

Uncovering the secrets of a healthy and happy life one tip at a time.

Hi, I'm Jen!

I believe everyone wants to live a more healthy life but let's face it, the world that we live in now doesn't always equip us well enough to do so. I decided to create The Wellness Code to have my own little online space to share the tips an tricks I've gathered to help me live as healthy and happy as possible. Welcome, and I hope you find some inspiration.
